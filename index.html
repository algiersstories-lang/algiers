Preserving the Memory of Algiers

This project is a digital tribute to Alger la Blanche. Every stone in the Casbah tells a story of dynasties, craftsmanship, and a Mediterranean identity that has endured for centuries. By documenting these monuments, we ensure the narrative of our ancestors remains alive for the next generation.
